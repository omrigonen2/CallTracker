What you can do with CallTracker

Whether you run ads, landing pages, or partner programs, the idea is the same: tie each inbound call back to a number and a campaign, then review outcomes in one place.

Campaigns & time-based routing

Assign tracking numbers to campaigns and set rules (like business hours) so calls reach the right destination—your team, a whisper prompt, or an IVR—without extra hardware.

Call log you can act on

See caller ID, duration, and status, listen to recordings when enabled, and add tags, notes, and outcomes (for example: converted, not relevant, spam) so your reporting stays honest.

Qualification & analytics

Mark calls that meet your length or quality bar and use dashboard analytics to understand answer rates and performance over time—per campaign, not in a spreadsheet.

Postbacks to your stack

Send call events (for example, when a call starts or completes) to your own URLs with retries, so you can connect CRM, ads, and internal tools without manual exports.

Teams & permissions

Multi-account SaaS with roles you control: invite people to the right workspace and limit who can change numbers, campaigns, or system settings.

English & Hebrew

The product supports English and Hebrew, including a layout that works correctly for right-to-left (RTL) users—not a bolt-on translation.
